About me

I am a Senior Computer Vision and Machine Learning Engineer & Research Scientist with over 20 years of professional experience developing high-impact Machine Vision solutions. My journey spans three continents: born and raised in Tunisia, I moved to the USA on a scholarship to purse my university studies at the University of Arizona, AZ, USA. After earning my Bachelor Degree, I came to Canada to purse my graduate studies at the University of Waterloo. I have been living in Canada for a long time, I speak English and French fluently, I am well-integrated and proud Canadian.


The highlights of my academic and professional are as follows:

  • Education:
    • BASc in Engineering and Mathematics | University of Arizona, AZ, USA.
    • MASc & PhD degrees in Applied Mathematics and Electrical & Computer Engineering | University of Waterloo, ON, Canada.
  • Professional Expertise:
    • Industrial AI: Imaging hardware optimization and selection, surface defect inspection, semiconductor manufacturing QA/QC, and automotive inspection damage detection.
    • Specialized Imaging: Industrial camera ISP pipelines, medical imaging, and satellite/environmental remote sensing, full-motion drone video processing and exploitation.
    • Security & Retail: Real-time surveillance analytics and retail shelf-monitoring.
    • Consumer Tech: Custom-fit wearables, industrial cameras and AI proof-of-concept and demonstrations.
  • Professional Development:
    • UBC Extended Learning: Currently enrolled in the Software Development Bootcamp and AI Application Development program.
    • Continuing Education: Regular completion of advanced AI/ML specialization courses on Coursera and Udemy to maintain expertise in emerging deep learning architectures.
